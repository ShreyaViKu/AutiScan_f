Emotion Recognition Game

How to Play

  • 👀 Look at the face
  • 🤔 Think about the emotion
  • 👉 Click the correct answer
  • ✅ Green = correct | ❌ Red = wrong
  • 🎯 Try to score maximum!

Why This Game Helps

  • 🧠 Improves emotional understanding
  • 👁 Enhances facial recognition skills
  • 💬 Helps social communication
  • 🎯 Builds focus and attention
  • 😊 Teaches empathy and feelings
