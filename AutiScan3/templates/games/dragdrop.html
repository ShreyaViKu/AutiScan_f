Drag & Drop Matching Game

How to Play

  • 👀 Look at the images carefully
  • 🖐 Drag the image
  • 📦 Drop it on the correct word
  • ✅ Green = correct | ❌ Red = wrong
  • 🎯 Try to match all correctly!

Why This Game Helps

  • 🧠 Improves memory and recognition skills
  • 👁 Enhances visual learning ability
  • 🖐 Develops motor coordination
  • 🎯 Builds focus and attention
  • 💡 Helps in understanding objects and language
