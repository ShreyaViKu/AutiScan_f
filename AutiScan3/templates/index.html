Autism Is a Spectrum

Autism is not a linear scale. It's a complex, multi-dimensional spectrum of traits that vary from person to person across different areas.

Social Interaction

Low
High

Difficulty with eye contact, reciprocity, or group interaction.

Communication

Low
High

Differences in verbal/non-verbal communication patterns.

Sensory Sensitivity

Low
High

Sensitivity to sound, light, textures, or environments.

Repetitive Behaviors

Low
High

Repetitive movements, routines, or restricted interests.

Focus & Attention

Low
High

Variability in sustained attention or hyperfocus.

Emotional Regulation

Low
High

Difficulty managing emotional responses.
